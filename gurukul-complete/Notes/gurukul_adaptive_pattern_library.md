# Gurukul Adaptive Pattern Library

### 1. Gentle Encouragement Pattern
**Trigger:** Dwell - Extended focus on content 
**UI Response:** Soft blue pulse around focused content, subtle warm glow that breathes gently  
**Why it exists:** Validates deep thinking and encourages persistence through challenging material without interrupting concentration  
**Gurukul Alignment Reasoning:** Honors the learner's natural pace and provides silent support, like a teacher's encouraging nod from across the room

### 2. Calm Clarity Pattern  
**Trigger:** Hover repeat - Multiple hovers over same options   
**UI Response:** Very gentle dimming of surrounding elements, soft focus ring around hovered item  
**Why it exists:** Reduces visual noise during decision-making moments, supporting thoughtful consideration over rushed choices  
**Gurukul Alignment Reasoning:** Quietly honors the learner’s thinking process, allowing ideas and understanding to unfold naturally without pressure.

### 3. Cognitive Relief Pattern
**Trigger:** Stress patterns - Rapid switching between elements, erratic mouse movement, multiple quick clicks  
**UI Response:** Gradual simplification of interface, non-essential elements fade to 40% opacity, breathing space increases  
**Why it exists:** Gently reduces visual clutter when the learner seems stressed, giving their mind room to settle and pay attention.
**Gurukul Alignment Reasoning:** Offers a peaceful moment amid complexity, helping the learner settle and concentrate on the key ideas naturally.

### 4. Rest Invitation Pattern
**Trigger:** Fatigue signals - Decreased interaction frequency, longer pauses between actions, repetitive scrolling  
**UI Response:** Subtle shift to warmer, softer colors, gentle suggestion of natural break points in content  
**Why it exists:** Acknowledges learning fatigue and gently guides toward restorative pauses without forcing interruption  
**Gurukul Alignment Reasoning:** Honors the natural rhythms of learning, recognizing that rest is essential for deep understanding

### 5. Gentle Wayfinding Pattern
**Trigger:** Backtrack - Repeated navigation between sections 
**UI Response:** Provides a calm sense of direction, softly highlighting where the learner is and where they can go next, letting them explore at their own pace.”
**Why it exists:** Supports non-linear learning exploration while providing orientation without judgment or pressure  
**Gurukul Alignment Reasoning:** Embraces the wandering nature of true learning, offering guidance while preserving autonomy and curiosity

### 6. Sacred Focus Pattern
**Trigger:** Deep focus protection - When the learner is fully absorbed in their learning, calmly exploring and engaging with the content   
**UI Response:** All non-essential UI elements gracefully fade away, creating a distraction-free learning sanctuary  
**Why it exists:** Protects and nurtures deep learning states by removing potential interruptions and distractions
