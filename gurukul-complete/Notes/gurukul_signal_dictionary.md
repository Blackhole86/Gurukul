# Gurukul Signal Dictionary

## CONFUSION_HIGH
Lots of help requests, re-reading, and slow responses in 5 minutes.
- **Trigger**: 3+ uncertainty signs in 5 minutes OR response time 200%+ slower
- **States**: confused, overloaded
- **Note**: Natural but needs help

## CONFUSION_PERSISTENT
Stuck and confused for a long time across multiple sections.
- **Trigger**: Confusion lasting 15+ minutes OR across 3+ sections
- **States**: confused, lost
- **Note**: May lead to giving up

## FOCUS_STABLE
Steady progress with good timing and accuracy.
- **Trigger**: Response times normal, accuracy 75%+, steady for 10+ minutes
- **States**: focused, flow
- **Note**: Don't interrupt this

## FOCUS_DROPPING
Starting to lose concentration and make more mistakes.
- **Trigger**: Response time up 50%+ OR accuracy down 20%+ OR less interaction
- **States**: fatigued, confused, overloaded
- **Note**: Catch this early

## FATIGUE_HIGH
Tired brain making mistakes on stuff they used to know.
- **Trigger**: Accuracy down 30%+ on familiar content OR very slow responses OR short sessions
- **States**: fatigued, overloaded
- **Note**: Time for a break

## FATIGUE_LOW
Fresh and alert, performing well.
- **Trigger**: Accuracy stable, normal timing, good session length
- **States**: focused, flow
- **Note**: Good to keep going

## NAVIGATION_LOOP
Going in circles, visiting same stuff repeatedly.
- **Trigger**: Same content 3+ times in 10 minutes OR circular browsing
- **States**: confused, lost, overloaded
- **Note**: They're lost and need direction

## INACTIVITY_LONG
Just sitting there, not doing anything.
- **Trigger**: No interaction for 3+ minutes OR no cursor movement for 2+ minutes
- **States**: fatigued, lost, overloaded
- **Note**: Something's wrong, check gently

## ERROR_RATE_HIGH
Making lots of mistakes.
- **Trigger**: 50%+ errors over 10 tries OR 3+ wrong answers in a row
- **States**: confused, overloaded, fatigued
- **Note**: Too hard or too tired

## PROGRESS_STALLED
Stuck in one place, not moving forward.
- **Trigger**: No progress for 15+ minutes OR repeated failed attempts
- **States**: confused, lost, overloaded
- **Note**: Need help to get unstuck

## ENGAGEMENT_DEEP
Really into it, exploring extra stuff voluntarily.
- **Trigger**: Session 150%+ longer than usual OR exploring 2+ optional things
- **States**: focused, flow
- **Note**: Stay invisible, don't break the magic

## RESPONSE_ERRATIC
All over the place - sometimes fast, sometimes slow, sometimes right, sometimes wrong.
- **Trigger**: Very inconsistent timing OR random right/wrong pattern
- **States**: overloaded, confused
- **Note**: System overload, simplify things
